Company Overview

Hajoka International Company Limited (HIC LTD) is an integrative international company 
incorporated on 17th August, 2012 with the certificate of incorporation number 93083 of 
Tanzania jurisdiction with the headquarters in Kahama, and other branches in North Mara 
and Geita  in the United Republic of Tanzania.
HIC LTD, among other objectives is mainly registered to undertake and executes Mechanical, 
Civil Engineering and Environment Control Contract Works. HIC LTD is registered under 
Contractors Registration Board (CRB) with Registration Numbers M5/37/10/2015 and 
C5/823/10/2015.

Mechanical Engineering:

Civil Engineering:

Environment Control:
